# Applications in Music Technology Using Midi Interface

## Abstract
This thesis explores applications of MIDI technology in music production and music technology systems. Emphasis is placed on the development, structure, and practical use of MIDI interfaces, as well as their role in contemporary music technology workflows.

## Thesis Structure
- **Chapter 1:** Introduction to MIDI and music technology applications
- **Chapter 2:** Methodology and applied sequential research approach
- **Chapter 3:** Literature review
- **Chapter 4:** Conclusions and findings
